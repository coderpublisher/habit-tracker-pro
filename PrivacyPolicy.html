Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 25, 2025

Welcome to HabitBuddy! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your habits, preferences, and analytics are stored locally on your device. We do not access, transmit, or process your data on any external servers.

2. App Permissions

  • Notifications: Used to remind you about your habits. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in your device settings.
  • Storage: Used for exporting/importing your habit data backups. No data leaves your device unless you choose to share or export it.

3. Analytics

HabitBuddy tracks basic usage statistics (like app opens, habit completions) only on your device to help you view your progress. This data is never sent to us or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

HabitBuddy does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. If you use the "Share App" feature, your device's sharing options are used, but no data is sent to us.

5. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on your device. We recommend using your device's built-in security features (such as passcodes or biometrics) to protect your information.
