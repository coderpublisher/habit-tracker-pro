Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I reset my habits?
    Go to the app settings and select "Reset Data" to clear all habits and progress.
  • How do I back up or restore my data?
    Use the "Export Data" and "Import Data" options in the app settings to back up or restore your habits.
  • How do I enable or disable notifications?
    Manage notification preferences in your device settings under "Notifications" for HabitBuddy.
  • Is my data private?
    Yes! All your data stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
